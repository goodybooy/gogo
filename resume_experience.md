# Resume — Experience Section (Draft)

## Quantitative Strategies, EM Flagship ($30B AUM)

Researched, enhanced, and productionized systematic alpha strategies underpinning the flagship $30B EM account; owned the full research cycle end-to-end — sourcing and cleaning data, signal construction, statistical validation, backtesting, risk sizing, and deployment into live trading.

- **CDX Momentum (EM Credit):** Developed and enhanced a systematic trend-following strategy on EM CDX indices, capturing directional risk-premium and persistence in sovereign credit. Engineered a PCA-based regime filter on cross-asset sentiment factors to dampen drawdowns in mean-reverting regimes; reduced realized volatility and improved risk-adjusted returns while preserving upside capture in trending environments.
- **EM FX Carry:** Built and refined a suite of EM-focused FX carry strategies, extending the baseline carry signal with credit-adjusted carry (CRAC), carry momentum variants, active/passive trend filters, and skew-based liquidity-provision overlays. Combined orthogonal factors into a unified signal to improve information ratio, reduce factor concentration, and harden performance through EM stress regimes.
- **Fair Value Spread (FVS) — EM Extension:** Contributed to extending PIMCO's structural fair-value framework (Merton-style distance-to-default → log-fit OAS) from DM into EM by incorporating country-specific covariates (sovereign OAS, rating, maturity, support uplift) into the FV regression, replacing static rating × maturity adjustment buckets with a dynamic country-aware specification. Applied across EM quasi-sovereign and corporate universes; built a production trading screener surfacing relative-value cheapness signals to PMs and traders for daily idea generation.

## Relative-Value Signal Generation, US Rates & EM Complex

Partnered with discretionary PMs to research and deploy a quantitative relative-value framework supporting structural trades across the US rates and EM complex. Designed a quant overlay that systematically extracts alpha on top of structural risk premia — combining CTA-style mean-reversion signals with fair-value modeling across a wide set of basis and curve trades — used to screen and validate discretionary trade theses, surface dislocations in real time, and serve as a benchmark to evaluate discretionary PM performance.

- **Mean-Reversion / Dislocation Monitor:** Built a suite of correlation- and z-score-based dislocation signals across FX-hedged yield differentials, bond rich/cheap switches, and DM/EM curve baskets, flagging short-horizon mispricings for tactical entry and PM idea generation.
- **Fair-Value Suite (Structural Basis):** Developed and maintained fair-value models across quasi-sovereign basis, CDS-cash basis, EUR-USD bond basis, corporate basis, and ASW spreads — using rolling regressions on country-specific credit, rate, and flow covariates to produce model-implied spreads, residual percentile-rank signals, and spread-duration-neutral hedging frameworks; ran daily monitors to surface relative cheapness/richness across the EM credit and rates universe.
- **PM Decision Support & Benchmarking:** Delivered the framework as a research utility for discretionary PMs — used to screen new trade ideas, stress-test thesis through quant-implied fair value, and benchmark realized PM performance against systematic baseline returns from the same signal set.

## Portfolio Analytics & Risk Research

Led portfolio-level analytics and risk research initiatives spanning machine learning, hedging efficiency, and proprietary curve infrastructure — delivering tools and studies that improved portfolio risk measurement, informed PM hedging decisions, and provided the analytical foundation for relative-value and smart-beta optimization workflows.

- **Machine Learning for Regime-Dependent Risk:** Designed and implemented machine-learning models to capture non-linear risk-factor behavior across distinct macro and volatility regimes, improving hedging efficiency and producing more accurate portfolio risk estimates than linear factor models — particularly in tail and transition states where standard sensitivities break down.
- **Cross-Desk Analytics Support:** Delivered ad-hoc and ongoing analytics across the desk, including tail-risk monitoring, tracking-error impact studies for proposed trades and rebalances, scenario and stress analysis on portfolio overlays, and bespoke risk decompositions for PM and risk-committee reviews.
- **Proprietary EM External Curve Library:** Built and maintained a proprietary curve library for emerging-market external debt, used as the firm-wide reference for spread-duration, OAS, and curve-shape analytics. The library upgraded portfolio-level risk measurement, served as the foundation for relative-value analysis across the EM credit universe, and provided the baseline curves used in smart-beta and systematic-overlay optimization.
