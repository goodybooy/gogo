
--- Pre-IPO Deal Source Research (Forge / HIIVE) ---

MAVEN VENTURES (https://www.mavenventures.com/)
- SF-based early-stage VC, founded 2013, ~$200M AUM (Fund IV $60M, closed Apr 2024)
- Strategy: concentrated bets on consumer software/technical founders
- 50 investments, 5 unicorn exits (Zoom, Cruise, Chariot, Perplexity, Wealthfront)
- Deal source on Forge/HIIVE likely = secondary sales from their portfolio companies
- Portfolio: https://www.mavenventures.com/portfolio
- Caplight profile: https://www.caplight.com/investor/mavenventures

CAPRICORN INVESTMENT GROUP (https://capricornllc.com/)
- Jeff Skoll (eBay co-founder) multi-family office, founded 2000, Palo Alto + NYC, ~$12.5B AUM
- Impact/ESG focus: ClimateTech, Energy Transition, Mobility, Circular Economy, Industrial Tech
- Invests via direct co-investments, SPVs, fund-of-funds, PE vehicles
- Recently opened to select new clients (previously Skoll-only)
- Notable public holdings: Joby Aviation, Planet Labs, QuantumScape, Navitas Semiconductor
- Deal source on Forge/HIIVE = secondary sales of their private holdings

FIRST TRUST CAPITAL SOLUTIONS — Private Assets Fund (FTPAX)
- Tender-offer fund specifically targeting pre-IPO equity + VC (growth equity, early-to-IPO stage)
- $12.5B AUM firm, available to Qualified Clients / Accredited Investors
- Min investment $50K (exceptions to $25K)
- Invests via primary fund commitments, direct co-investments, secondaries
- NAV calc moved from quarterly → monthly as of Jul 31 2025
- This is the most direct "deal source" product — likely the Forge/HIIVE listings
